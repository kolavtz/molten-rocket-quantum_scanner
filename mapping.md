Notes / next steps
- The primary authoritative model mapping is in `src/models.py` (Certificate, CBOMEntry, PQCClassification, DomainCurrentState, AssetSSLProfile). Use that as the canonical source when creating migrations.
- The two ingestion paths are: (1) `SSLCaptureService` (ORM-based) and (2) `src/scanner/ssl_worker.py` (raw SQL). Both must be kept semantically identical: dedup selection logic, is_current handling, certificate_details handling, and current state updates.
